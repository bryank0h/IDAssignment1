City-building strategy game "Simp City" ----- 2021


This game is created as a final assignment upon learning the basics of the Python Programming Language.

The player is the mayor of a city known as "Simp City", and the goal of the game is to build the happiest and most prosperous city possible.

The player's main objective is to score the most points possible by building among 5 different buildings in 16 turns. Each building's placement has an effect on the amount of points earned, hence this places the player's strategic mind to the test in order to score the most points.
