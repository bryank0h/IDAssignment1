About Me

The challenge of learning various programming languages reminds and motivates me that I still have a long road ahead of me and never to throw in the towel easily. With a constant drive to pursue my goals and gaining experiences such as from an internship, I shall be equipped with the necessary skills and knowledge to excel in what I do.
